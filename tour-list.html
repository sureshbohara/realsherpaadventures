Everest Trips

The Everest region is one of the most spectacular trekking destinations for all the adventurous in the world, Everest region also well known as the Khumbu region which lies in the highest alpine zone with the highest mountain in the world Mt. Everest (8848M) and other several mountains over 8000m, like- Lhotse, Cho Oyu, and Makalu and many others mountain under seven thousand meters hight, while trekking in this area you will also have a really good opportunity to explore the mountain ecosystem, sherpa people, tradition, monasteries, superb geographical part of Solukhumbu and observing wide varieties of plants and animals, glaciers, forests and flora, and fauna as well as the largest Glacier in the world the Khumbu glacier and Khumbu Ice Fall from Everest Base Camp makes one unbelievable your entire trip in Khumbu region. The ultimate Himalaya experience with your local travel partner.

The Everest region trek starts from easy to strenuous. Eventually, Trekking to the Everest region requires very good health as you wish to accomplish the goal and make a lifetime experience of the Himalaya. Alpine Ramble has a perfect itinerary for all the trip packages in the Everest region where we have included some extra day rest for acclimatization day to complete the trip without any problems. Our Alpine Ramble team will customize your trip itinerary according to your budget and time. Please feel free to join us for any of your suitable trips on your holidays.

We Alpine Ramble Treks offer various trip itineraries for the Everest region, please share your interest with your local travel partner, we also customize your trip itinerary according to your time and budget.
